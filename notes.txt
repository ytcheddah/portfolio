HTML notes

    <header> tag creates a section that is a header. Typically you want this section 
    to be your navigation bar and logo. Make it nice and shiny cause this is the 
    first thing visitors will see. 
                    
                        Keywords: Straightforward, impactful!

    <section> tag with an id of about. Personal introduction. Brief bio and a photo.

    <article> or <div> tags separate and organize projects.

    <section> tag with id of contact. Should be a simple form for potential customers 
    to be able to contact you at.

    <footer> section for additional information. Socials, copyright, relavent info
